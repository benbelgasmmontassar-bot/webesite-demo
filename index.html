A Letter to You

Four years. Four years of laughing at the same things, of quiet evenings that somehow become the best memories, of choosing each other every single day — even on the hard ones.

You have a way of making the ordinary feel like something worth keeping forever. The way you smile when you think nobody is watching. The way you make every room feel warmer just by being in it.

I do not have the right words to tell you what these four years have meant to me. But I hope you feel it — in every little thing, in every quiet moment — how deeply and completely you are loved.

Here is to every year still ahead of us. Here is to you, always.

— Yours, forever 🤍
